#Physics 
## $\displaystyle \hat{H}\Psi= i\hbar \frac{ \partial \Psi }{ \partial t }$
* $\displaystyle \hat{H}$ is the [[Hamiltonian Operator]]
* $\displaystyle \Psi$ is $\displaystyle \Psi(\vec{r},t)$
* $\displaystyle \hbar$ is the [[reduced Planck constant]]
