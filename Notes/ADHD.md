#Medicine 
A neurodevelopmental disorder that results in a loss in executive functioning. This can mean problems with planning, maintaining attention on tasks, impulsivity, hyperactivity, etc. In day-to-day life, this might manifest as problems with deadlines, time blindness, paying attention during class or during conversations
## Subtypes
* Hyperactive-impulsive (ADHD-HI)
* Inattentive (ADHD-I)
* Combined (ADHD-C)
