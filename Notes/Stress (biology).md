#Psychology 
## Stress Stages
* Primary Appraisal. Identify if threat is:
	* Irrelevant
	* Benign
	* Stressful. Then apply secondary appraisal, where you assess whether you can handle the threat
## Stressors
* Environmental
* Daily Events
* Workplace or academic setting
* Social expectations
* Chemical/biological stressors
* Pressure
* Frustration
